[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/01/anthropic-ipo-s1-prospectus.html) · [NBC](https://www.nbcnews.com/business/corporations/anthropic-files-ipo-openai-rcna347897) · [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/06/01/tech/anthropic-ipo-filing)

  *Why now:* Every CIO objection about Anthropic capacity, durability or "is this thing a real company" just evaporated — and Anthropic's enterprise team is about to be soaked up by the IPO roadshow.
  *Angle:* Re-open every paused Claude PoC this week with a one-pager citing the S-1 + $965B + chip-partner stack; pre-book Q3 capacity and a Q4 production date before bench supply tightens around the listing.

Runs on Maia silicon. [Cybernews](https://cybernews.com/ai-news/microsoft-new-homegrown-ai-models-copilot-github/) · [ChatForest](https://chatforest.com/builders-log/microsoft-build-2026-recap-windows-agent-platform-project-polaris-copilot-workspace/) · [AI Weekly](https://aiweekly.co/alerts/microsoft-targets-claude-code-with-project-polaris)

  *Why now:* Every Copilot Enterprise tenant has 90 days to evaluate Polaris and decide whether to migrate, dual-run or hold on GPT-4 — that's a billable assessment with a hard August deadline.
  *Angle:* Sell a fixed-price "Polaris readiness audit" — 2-week sprint, sample 50 repos, score regression risk, deliver a migration plan and a benchmarked SLA on a named language (Rust / Haskell / Python). Open the door on a follow-on managed migration.

  *Why now:* Indian enterprises just got data-resident GPT capacity and TCS is being asked to deliver more demand than its bench can absorb — which is where mid-tier subcontractors land.
  *Angle:* Position as a TCS / Infosys subcontractor for OpenAI-stack delivery on time-and-materials — fixed Lahore/Karachi rate card, dollar-billed, lighter than TCS overhead, fronted by a Bangalore PM.

  *Why now:* Connector inventory is at 1,400 and growing, but per-customer agent deployments still need integrators — Azure sellers will compensate partners on agents this fiscal year.
  *Angle:* Productise a "1 Foundry agent live in 30 days, $25K fixed" SKU — pick one of the top-10 connectors (SAP S/4 invoice triage, Salesforce lead-to-account, Dynamics returns), ship to GA, charge a recurring tuning retainer.

  *Why now:* Devin's sales motion is short on humans-in-the-loop for regulated workloads, and 50% MoM growth means rollouts are sprinting past the customer success bench.
  *Angle:* Sell a "Devin pilot pod" — 2 senior engineers + 1 SRE who own the Devin queue for one regulated team for 60 days, output a verified PR throughput number, and stay as a retained ops partner.
