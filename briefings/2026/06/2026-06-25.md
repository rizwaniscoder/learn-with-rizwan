# Daily AI Brief — June 25, 2026
*~5 min read · 19 items · Thursday*

> OpenAI's first chip dropped yesterday — Jalapeño, an LLM-tuned inference ASIC co-built with Broadcom in a record nine-month design cycle, ~50% lower cost-per-token than current GPUs, first racks landing end of 2026 and a roadmap to 10GW by 2029. Combined with Vishal Sikka's $32M Hang Ten launch (an AI-native challenger to TCS/Infosys/Accenture) and Anthropic poaching two more senior Gemini staffers (four in six days), the buyer side of AI services repriced in 24 hours: NVIDIA isn't the only inference path, the Big 3 SIs aren't the only delivery option, and Google isn't the only home for top researchers.

## 🎯 Opportunity signals
*For founders and service businesses.*

- **Jalapeño breaks the "NVIDIA or bust" assumption — second-source AI advisory just became a CIO line item.**
  *What happened:* OpenAI and Broadcom unveiled Jalapeño on June 24, OpenAI's first custom inference accelerator. Reticle-sized ASIC, nine months from design to tape-out (the fastest high-performance ASIC cycle on record), targeting ~50% lower cost-per-token than the GPUs OpenAI runs today. First deployments end of 2026, scaling to 10GW by 2029 ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/openai-broadcom-jalapeno-inference-chip/), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-24/openai-and-broadcom-unveil-ai-chip-to-run-models-faster-cheaper), [Tom's Hardware](https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/artificial-intelligence/broadcom-and-openai-unveil-custom-built-jalapeno-inference-processor-openais-first-chip-is-a-massive-reticle-sized-asic-built-in-an-ultra-fast-nine-month-development-cycle)).
  *Who can act on it:* MLOps boutiques, FinOps consultancies, anyone who already runs eval harnesses across multiple model providers.
  *Why now:* Every Fortune 1000 just got a new "what's our inference roadmap?" question from the board, and the answer is no longer "buy more H100s." With Google TPU v8, AWS Trainium 3, AMD MI400 and Jalapeño all credible by Q1, the matrix of inference options doubled overnight.
  *Angle:* Productize a 3-week "Inference Cost Audit" — pull last 90 days of API spend, model the same workload on 4 hardware/provider combinations, deliver a board-ready migration plan. Fixed fee $25–60k. Lead generation: target any company with >$2M/year in OpenAI/Anthropic invoices.

- **Vishal Sikka's Hang Ten Systems ($32M seed) declares war on TCS/Infosys/Accenture — Pakistan shops have a 12-month window to do the same.**
  *What happened:* Former Infosys CEO Vishal Sikka launched Hang Ten Systems with a $32M seed led by Mayfield (Aramco Ventures + angels; Jerry Yang on the board) on June 24. Pitch: AI-native enterprise software delivery using agentic code generation and reusable skill libraries, replacing the 100k-headcount labor model. Already shipping for Siemens Gamesa and Fresenius after one month of existence ([Entrackr](https://entrackr.com/news/former-infosys-ceo-vishal-sikkas-hang-ten-systems-raises-32-mn-seed-round-12069612), [SaaS News](https://www.thesaasnews.com/news/hang-ten-systems-raises-32m-seed/)).
  *Who can act on it:* Karachi/Lahore/Islamabad shops with US/UK/GCC clients; ex-Systems-Limited / 10Pearls / Folio3 talent thinking about going independent.
  *Why now:* India's top IT firms net-hired 17 people across the first 9 months of FY26 — the entry-level pipeline has collapsed and clients know it. The "AI-native delivery boutique" category now has a brand-name reference customer; window is open before the second wave of US-funded clones lands.
  *Angle:* Reposition around 2 productized outcomes ("ship a production agent in 30 days," "modernize one legacy app/quarter") priced at 50–70% of Hang Ten / EPAM rate cards. Use the Siemens Gamesa case as social proof in outbound — "we deliver the same outcome at a third of the cost."

- **UAE AI hiring grew 48% YoY (top global market) — the highest signal-to-noise outbound geography on the planet.**
  *What happened:* AI-related hiring in the UAE accelerated from 32% in 2023–24 to 48% in 2024–25, the strongest growth rate of any tracked market globally. UAE ranks #1 in employer hiring sentiment with 56% planning expansion; 45–75% report unfilled AI roles ([Gulf Business](https://gulfbusiness.com/en/2026/jobs/ai-engineers-wanted-uae-becomes-global-hiring-hotspot/), [VBeyond](https://www.vbeyond.com/blog/strategic-horizon-2026-the-gcc-labor-market-transformation-report/)).
  *Who can act on it:* Pakistan/India/MENA agencies, freelancer collectives, AI training providers, any firm that can deliver in GST timezone with English contracts.
  *Why now:* Two-year UAE Golden Visa for AI specialists is now de-facto policy, and budget cycles for FY27 lock by September. A pitch this quarter lands inside the new fiscal year's signed PO.
  *Angle:* Pick one Dubai/Abu Dhabi vertical (banking, real estate, healthcare, government services) and produce 3 vertical-specific case studies in 30 days — even from pro-bono pilots. Cold outreach to CTOs of DIFC-listed firms with a fixed-fee discovery sprint.

- **Anthropic absorbed two more senior Gemini researchers in 24 hours (four in six days) — the recruiting wedge for talent shops just narrowed.**
  *What happened:* Jonas Adler (Google's coding-AI lead) and Alexander Pritzel (AlphaFold/training infra) confirmed moves to Anthropic on June 24 — the fourth and fifth senior DeepMind/Gemini departures in a week, following Noam Shazeer and John Jumper ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-24/google-poised-to-lose-two-more-high-profile-ai-staffers-to-anthropic), [Neowin](https://www.neowin.net/amp/google-reportedly-set-to-lose-two-key-gemini-and-deepmind-researchers-to-anthropic/)).
  *Who can act on it:* AI talent firms, exec-recruiting boutiques, technical-staffing shops.
  *Why now:* Pre-IPO equity arbitrage (Anthropic at $965B private valuation, OpenAI IPO targeting September) makes every senior Google AI hire a "would you take the call?" candidate. The wave is real and self-reinforcing — the next 90 days are when Tier-2 Google researchers start fielding inbound.
  *Angle:* Build a curated list of 50 Tier-2 Google/DeepMind ICs (L5–L6) and run a no-fee "career mapping" outreach. Even a 10% conversion rate to retained search at frontier labs or post-Series-B startups books a quarter's revenue.

- **Taktile ($110M Series C, Goldman Sachs-led) opens "regulated-vertical agent integration" as a category.**
  *What happened:* Berlin/NY-based Taktile raised $110M Series C at undisclosed valuation, led by Goldman Sachs Alternatives with Tiger Global, Index, Y Combinator. Product: "Agentic Decision Platform" — orchestrates LLM agents + rules + human review for loan approvals, claims, fraud, underwriting. One major insurer customer projecting $90M/year in claims-processing savings ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/06/24/exclusive-taktile-goldman-sachs-ai-bank-insurance-funding/), [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/news/investment-tracker/2026/taktile-raises-110-million-to-automate-high-stakes-banking-and-insurance-decisions/)).
  *Who can act on it:* Regulated-industry implementation shops; ex-Big-4 risk-and-compliance consultants going independent.
  *Why now:* Goldman leading the round legitimizes "agents in regulated workflows" for every bank, insurer and broker board. Taktile is the platform; nobody has the integration channel.
  *Angle:* Become the certified Taktile integration partner before there are 50 of them. Run a 4-week pilot ("automate one decision pathway") at fixed fee, then move to revenue share on processing-volume savings.

- **Assort Health hit $1.2B at $120M Series C — vertical-AI agent playbook is now copy-paste for every healthcare-adjacent niche.**
  *What happened:* Assort Health closed $120M Series C led by Menlo Ventures at $1.2B valuation on June 24. Revenue 20x in 15 months; platform "Synapse" trained on 190M patient voice interactions, 62k care protocols, 1.6M decision pathways ([Menlo](https://menlovc.com/perspective/keeping-the-front-door-of-healthcare-open-our-investment-in-assort-health/), [HIT Consultant](https://hitconsultant.net/2026/06/24/assort-health-raises-120-million-series-c-menlo/)).
  *Who can act on it:* Vertical-AI founders in dental, vet, optometry, behavioral health, legal intake, real-estate brokerages — anyone with a phone-driven appointment-and-intake workflow.
  *Why now:* Menlo just told the market "the patient-call agent is a unicorn category." Adjacent verticals will draft off the proof for the next 24 months.
  *Angle:* Pick one phone-heavy vertical with ≥10k US-based small businesses, ship a working voice-agent prototype in 30 days, sell it sub-$1k/mo to 5 lighthouse customers. Either flips into a venture pitch or generates SMB-services cash flow.
