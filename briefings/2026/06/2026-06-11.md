- **EU AI Act transparency obligations bite August 2 — 7 weeks out.**
  *What happened:* Most AI Act provisions apply August 2, 2026, including transparency duties.   *Angle:* Sell a fixed-scope "AI Act Article 50 transparency readiness" sprint — inventory, labeling, watermarking, deployer disclosures — to firms in DACH and Benelux.
