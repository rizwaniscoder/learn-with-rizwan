  *Angle:* productize a fixed-price "Claude SMB Setup" ($2–5k) targeting US accounting firms, e-commerce, professional services — sell via Upwork plus cold outbound to tour-city attendees.

  *Angle:* sell "UAE-timezone AI engineering pods" via a UAE EOR (Deel, RemotePass) at 40% of local cost — target Series A/B Dubai startups and UAE bank innovation arms.
