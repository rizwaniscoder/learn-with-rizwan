*For founders and service businesses.*

- **Salesforce Hosted MCP Servers hit GA — instant agent channel into every Enterprise org.**
  *What happened:* Salesforce made Hosted MCP Servers generally available, included free in every Enterprise Edition org and above.   *Who can act on it:* Salesforce consultancies, Agentforce specialists, MCP build shops with CRM domain depth.
  *Why now:* the GA flip means thousands of Salesforce-heavy SMBs and mid-market shops can now plug agents into their CRM with zero infra work — but they need someone to design the MCP surface and the agent flows. Almost no one has done it on their stack yet.
  *Angle:* package a "30-day Agentforce + MCP audit" — map the customer's standard objects + Apex actions to a custom MCP server, ship one production agent (lead triage, renewal forecast, support assistant). Bill $25-40K.

  *Who can act on it:* services shops, Upwork-grade independents, dev studios that can stand up "FDE-as-a-service" pods.
  *Why now:* OpenAI alone is hiring ~12 people/day to reach 8,000 by year-end with most new hires going to enterprise deployment — they can't fill it onshore. Anthropic, Mistral, and the foundation labs are in the same race.
  *Angle:* productize a 2-3 person FDE pod (1 senior, 1 mid, 1 junior, $18K-$28K/month) and pitch directly to the partner-success teams at Anthropic, Mistral, Cohere, and the Big 5 AI consultancies.
  [source](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2026/01/ai-has-already-added-1-3-million-new-jobs-according-to-linkedin-data/)

- **Nvidia just made its first legal-AI bet — vertical-agent buildouts are the new wedge.**

  *Why now:* Nvidia's check is the loudest market signal yet that vertical AI is where the durable margins are. The same playbook (buy domain experts → fine-tune → ship workflow agents) is wide open in healthcare, construction, accounting, insurance.
  *Angle:* pick one underserved vertical (e.g., GCC real-estate law, US construction subs, Pakistani textile compliance), build one workflow agent on Claude/Kimi K2.6, sign three lighthouse customers, then raise pre-seed off the wedge.
  [source](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/30/legal-ai-startup-legora-hits-5-6-valuation-and-its-battle-with-harvey-just-got-hotter/)
