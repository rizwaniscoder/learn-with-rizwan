# Daily AI Brief — July 09, 2026
*~5 min read · 18 items · Thursday*

> OpenAI ships GPT-5.6 Sol/Terra/Luna to the public today — the same week SpaceXAI released Grok 4.5 into Cursor and Anthropic's Fable 5 came back off the export-restricted list. For the first time in a month, every frontier lab has a shipping model at the same time, and the question flips from "which model can I get" to "which one is worth migrating to." Pricing crashed with it: Terra at $2.50/$15, Grok 4.5 at $2/$6, Fable 5 back at parity — Opus 4.8 is suddenly the most expensive tier by a wide margin.

Frame it as "certified before your competitors even start."
