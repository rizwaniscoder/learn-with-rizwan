## 🎯 Opportunity signals

- **EU Code of Practice signing closes tomorrow, 18:00 CEST — Article 50 goes live in 12 days.**
  *What happened:* The Commission's Code of Practice on Transparency of AI-Generated Content closes to initial signatories at 6pm CEST on July 22. Signing buys a presumption of conformity with Article 50(2) and 50(4), moving the evidentiary burden to regulators. Article 50 (chatbot disclosure, deepfake labeling, machine-readable watermarking on synthetic audio/image/video/text) becomes enforceable August 2, 2026. Fines: up to €15M or 3% of global turnover.
  *Who can act on it:* Compliance and product teams that can wire disclosure UI, watermarking and logging into shipping products this week.
  *Why now:* 24 hours to sign, 12 days to enforcement. Legacy systems get until Dec 2 to retrofit, but new deployments must comply from Aug 2.
  *Angle:* Same-day service: "we file your CNECT signatory form tonight and map obligations to your stack." Follow-on: €5K fixed-price 5-day Article 50 readiness audit shipped before August 2.
