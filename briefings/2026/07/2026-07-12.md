# Daily AI Brief — July 12, 2026
*~5 min read · 20 items · Sunday*

> Apple sued OpenAI in the Northern District of California on Friday for systematic trade-secret theft "at every level" — and the same week confirmed Siri will run on Google Gemini, not ChatGPT — a clean break with the 2024 partnership on the same day SK Hynix's $26.5B Nasdaq debut priced the AI memory stack at $1.27T and Meta shipped its first *paid* frontier API.

## 🎯 Opportunity signals
*For founders and service businesses.*

- **Apple ↔ OpenAI partnership is over — Siri moves to Gemini, hardware race turns litigious**
  *What happened:* Apple filed suit against OpenAI on July 10 alleging its Chief Hardware Officer Tang Tan (ex-Apple VP) directed recruits to bring confidential documents on unreleased iPhone/Apple Watch products to job interviews, and that former Apple engineer Chang Liu downloaded "dozens" of confidential hardware files before leaving in January 2026. Apple simultaneously confirmed the revamped Siri will use Google Gemini, ending the 2024 ChatGPT-in-iOS partnership.
  *Who can act on it:* iOS/Apple-ecosystem shops with any Gemini or Vertex experience, and mobile-AI integrators serving Fortune 500 apps that will now have to plan for a Gemini-in-Siri surface.
  *Why now:* The Siri-Gemini transition kicks off a wave of iOS app updates that need to route AI queries through Gemini APIs or on-device Apple Foundation Models instead of ChatGPT. Enterprise iOS teams have 6-9 months of remediation work coming.
  *Angle:* Package a "Siri/Gemini migration audit" — 2-week scoped engagement to inventory an iOS app's OpenAI touchpoints and produce a Gemini/Foundation Models rewrite plan, priced $18-30k, upsell into implementation retainers. [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/apple-openai-lawsuit-trade-secrets.html) · [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2026/07/10/apple-sues-openai-alleging-ai-company-stole-trade-secrets/)

- **Meta opens its first paid API — new evaluation and migration wedge**
  *What happened:* Meta Superintelligence Labs shipped Muse Spark 1.1 on July 9, a multimodal reasoning model with a 1M-token context, and — for the first time in company history — put it on a *paid* Meta Model API at $1.25/$4.25 per M tokens with $20 free credits. Meta is positioning it against GPT-5.5, Opus 4.8 and Gemini 3.1 Pro.
  *Who can act on it:* Any shop that already sells LLM evaluation, routing, or migration work.
  *Why now:* Meta's paid API triples the number of frontier vendors buyers can shortlist. Every enterprise procurement team now has a new "should we add Meta?" question to answer, and Meta's aggressive $1.25 input price makes routing a live cost-optimization pitch.
  *Angle:* Sell a 3-week "5-vendor eval sprint" (OpenAI/Anthropic/xAI/Google/Meta) — canonical eval harness, per-task cost/latency table, routing PoC — priced $30-50k; add a retainer to run the harness monthly. [Meta AI](https://ai.meta.com/blog/introducing-muse-spark-meta-model-api/) · [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/09/meta-enters-the-crowded-ai-coding-battle-with-muse-spark-1-1/)

- **China's Anthropomorphic AI rules go into effect July 15 — 3 days out**
  *What happened:* The Cyberspace Administration of China's Interim Measures for AI Anthropomorphic Interactive Services take effect Wednesday. They cover any service that "simulates the personality, thinking patterns and communication styles of natural persons for continuous emotional interaction" — companion apps, virtual partners, character chatbots — with hard bans on virtual intimate relationships for minors, mandatory guardian consent under 14, extreme-emotion detection, and prohibited self-harm/suicide content.
  *Who can act on it:* AI product studios and safety consultancies serving Chinese platforms, EU character-AI startups with China exposure, and global companion-app operators (Character.AI, Replika-style) that route any traffic through Chinese infrastructure or serve Chinese users.
  *Why now:* Fixed statutory date, no grace period. Existing consumer AI companion apps need minor-mode gates, extreme-emotion detection classifiers, and tiered intervention flows in production by Wednesday.
  *Angle:* Ship a "China Anthropomorphic AI readiness pack" — pre-built minor-mode logic, self-harm classifier calibration, guardian-consent flow, and audit report — priced $25-40k, delivered in 5 days as an emergency SOW. [Bird & Bird](https://www.twobirds.com/en/insights/2026/china/china%27s-new-regulations-on-ai-anthropomorphic-interactive-services) · [Hogan Lovells](https://www.hoganlovells.com/en/publications/chinas-interim-measures-for-the-administration-of-anthropomorphic-ai-interaction-services)

- **The Fed puts Andreessen on an AI-and-jobs task force reporting by year-end**
  *What happened:* Fed Chair Kevin Warsh announced five external task forces on July 9; a16z co-founder Marc Andreessen will co-lead the Productivity and Jobs panel with Stanford's Charles I. Jones and Microsoft EVP Asha Sharma. Mandate: assess AI's impact on productivity, employment and monetary policy; deliver recommendations by end-2026.
  *Who can act on it:* US policy-adjacent consultancies, workforce-transformation advisories, and services shops selling AI ROI/productivity measurement.
  *Why now:* A Fed task force headed by the most bullish AI VC in the country is a strong forward signal that "AI + productivity" narrative will dominate US enterprise investment through year-end. CFOs are about to be asked to quantify AI ROI in earnings calls.
  *Angle:* Package an "AI productivity attribution study" — 4 weeks, embed with a client's finance team, produce a defensible per-dollar/per-headcount AI value estimate that survives Q3 earnings scrutiny — priced $60-90k. [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/2026/07/09/federal-reserve-enlists-marc-andreessen-advise-ai-under-warsh/) · [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/07/09/fed-warsh-task-force-andreessen)

- **Ollama closes $65M Series B — self-host / on-prem AI is going pro**

  *Angle:* Sell a fixed-price "sovereign inference in 30 days" SOW — Ollama or vLLM behind a client's VPC, one open-weight model of the client's choice, a monitored eval harness, one production workflow migrated — priced $45-65k, add managed retainer. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/09/popular-open-source-ai-developer-tool-ollama-raises-65m-grows-to-nearly-9m-users/)
